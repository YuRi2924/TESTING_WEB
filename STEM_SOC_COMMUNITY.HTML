STEM society organization

This section highlights the diverse organizations within the STEM community, each contributing uniquely to the advancement of the field. It provides an overview of their missions, initiatives, and roles in fostering collaboration, innovation, and academic growth. These organizations empower members to actively engage in research, development, and community-building activities, advancing science, technology, engineering, and mathematics in meaningful ways.

STEM SOCIETY

The STEM Society is the central organization of the University of the Cordilleras Integrated School, dedicated to creating engaging and enriching campus activities. It organizes a variety of events such as the STEM Opening Assembly, the Mr. and Ms. STEM, Katangkays tutorial, and STEM Night.

ARTIFICIAL UNIVERSE GUILD

The Artificial Universe Guild is dedicated to exploring the vast world of technology and physics, with a focus on areas such as robotics, computer programming, and electronics. The guild aims to foster innovation and creativity among its members by providing hands-on experience, collaborative projects, and access to cutting-edge tools. Through workshops, competitions, and real-world applications, the guild strives to inspire the next generation of tech pioneers and problem-solvers.

NUMERICAL JAGUAR GUILD

The Numerical Jaguar Guild delves into the fascinating world of mathematics, exploring its diverse branches and real-world applications. Through collaborative problem-solving, competitions, and engaging discussions, the guild fosters a deeper understanding of mathematical concepts and their significance in various fields. The guild aims to inspire a passion for mathematics, encourage critical thinking, and equip its members with the skills to tackle complex challenges, both within academia and beyond.

UNIVERSITY OF THE CORDILLERAS SENOIR PLUS YOUTH COUNCIL

The University of the Cordilleras Senior Plus Youth Council focuses on the well-being of students, emphasizing the importance of health and safety on campus. The council provides essential first aid training, equipping students with the knowledge and skills to respond effectively in emergencies.

Trailblazers' Hall

The Trailblazers' Hall celebrates STEM students who excel in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. It honors those who demonstrate exceptional dedication and leadership, proving that success in STEM extends beyond the classroom. These students inspire others with their commitment and achievements, shaping the future both in academia and in the community.
