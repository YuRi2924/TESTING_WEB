ABOUT US

The STEM Society at the University of the Cordilleras Integrated School in Baguio City fosters a passion for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) through innovative projects, collaborative learning, and hands-on activities. Committed to academic excellence and community-driven initiatives, it empowers students to explore their potential, engage in robotics competitions, research, and technology-focused events, and prepare as future leaders and innovators.

ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

This website is developed to support the STEM students of the University of the Cordilleras in their academic journey. It offers three main features: the Research Archives, the STEM Community, and Practice Quizzes, each designed to enhance students' learning experience and foster a collaborative environment.
